Who I Am

Hi, I'm Katie Owens! I am 20 years old from county Cavan, Ireland. I am the youngest of three in my family. I have an older sister, Aine who is 26 and an older brother, Michael who is 23. I am currently a second year student in Dublin City University, studying Marketing Innovation and Technology. For my Web Design module, we have been instructed to design a website about anything and so, I decided to create a personal blog as I thoroughly enjoy reading other people's. This is going to be a lifestyle blog, a place for me to share my thoughts, experiences, and ideas. From fashion to sports, I enjoy delving into topics that excite me and hopefully you will feel the same way about them! When I'm not writing, you can find me in the gym or on a shopping trip with my mum and sister.

Why I Started This Blog

As i mentioned previously, I created this blog as part of my Web Design module. However, writing a blog is always something i wanted to do as it is a great way to connect with like-minded people who share the same ideas and interests as me. I have always kept a journal as i believe writing is an incredible way for me to express myself. Thanks for stopping by!
